About Us

At Afews Aqua, we are committed to transforming aquaculture with science-backed, sustainable solutions. Our premium-grade products are designed to support shrimp and fish farming across every stage — from water quality management to gut health and immunity.

Our formulations are developed through rigorous research and tested in real aquaculture environments. We blend advanced microbiology, essential trace minerals, and probiotics to ensure healthy aquatic ecosystems and improved yield for farmers.

Our mission is to empower aquaculture communities with trusted, eco-friendly products that ensure safety, performance, and growth. We believe in long-term partnerships, transparency, and driving innovation for a better aquatic future.
